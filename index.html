Jose Garcia

Jose is an Operations Supervisor at the Regional Compost Facility in Vernon, British Columbia. He has also been an environmental scientist, project manager and business owner. Jose has an academic background in environmental science and engineering and holds Master of Applied Science degree in Civil Engineering and a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science degree. He has more than 16 years of experience in the environmental/sustainability fields, most recently managing organics recycling and biosolids composting operations. As a consultant he helped clients assess properties and manage environmental liabilities in compliance with government regulatory requirements. Over his career, Jose has managed and successfully delivered over 100 projects for a wide range of clients including private individuals, commercial enterprises, industrial manufacturers, insurance and lending institutions, Indigenous communities, municipal and regional governments and utilities. Jose helped develop the Sustainable Winegrowing British Columbia program from 2008 to 2011.
